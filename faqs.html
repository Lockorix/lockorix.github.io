Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Answers Regarding On-Site Technical Services, Security, and Scheduling

Have questions about how we handle technical support, security implementations, or local service visits? We believe in absolute transparency. Below are answers to the most common questions we receive from homes, small businesses, and healthcare practices.

Do you have a physical office or repair storefront?

No, we do not operate a retail storefront, and services are not performed at a Lockorix office. We operate as a modern, fully mobile technical unit. **Lockorix comes entirely to you.** This saves you the time and hassle of disconnecting your office workstations, bundle cables, or pack up home routers just to get a technician to look at them.

What areas do you physically travel to for support?

Our standard mobile dispatch zone covers Kansas City (KCK & KCMO), Overland Park, Lawrence, Topeka, and Liberty, Missouri—along with all the communities in between. If your home or workplace sits within this regional corridor, we can easily coordinate an on-site visit.

Can my technical issue be resolved remotely instead?

Yes! For software patches, configuration errors, email filter setups, and system checkups, we can establish an encrypted, temporary remote session. This lets us log in securely and fix bugs instantly without making you wait for a technician to drive over. If the problem turns out to be hardware failure or a dropped network, we will shift immediately to an on-site visit.

How does Lockorix address service contracts and legal parameters?

We handle legal and operational agreements on a highly practical, case-by-case basis. Initial consultations or simple, isolated hardware fixes do not require long-term contracts. For clients requiring ongoing managed infrastructure or recurring cyber defense, we will cross-execute a tailored Master Services Agreement (MSA) or Statement of Work (SOW) to protect your liability before major changes are made.

How do you handle sensitive data and compliance requirements?

Data integrity is our absolute specialty. For medical and corporate clients, our processes feature a zero-local-storage data tracking architecture. We use hardware-based Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), utilize full disk encryption on all technical endpoints, and legally sign necessary Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) to fulfill strict compliance guidelines prior to accessing secure systems.

How do I schedule an on-site visit or a technical discovery call?

Scheduling is entirely streamlined. You can pick an exact date and time slot that fits your calendar via our Calendly Scheduling Page. Alternatively, if you prefer to share a summary of your technical issues or setup goals first, you can drop us a line using our digital Service Intake Form, and we will contact you within 24 business hours.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Lockorix Security is a formally registered, licensed, and fully insured Limited Liability Company (LLC) based out of Kansas City, Kansas. We hold comprehensive commercial protections so you can have complete peace of mind while we work on your physical computers and internal networks.
