Privacy Policy & Data Protection Standards

Last Updated: June 2026 | Our Commitments to Residential, Corporate, and Healthcare Clients

At Lockorix Security, we realize that providing technical support, network monitoring, and cyber defense requires an exceptionally high level of trust. Whether we are hardening a home wireless network, maintaining hardware for a small business, or optimizing a medical practice, safeguarding your confidential operational records and personal details is built directly into our core daily workflows.

1. Information Collected & Source

We limit our data collection strictly to what is necessary to evaluate your technical needs, schedule consultations, and deliver secure IT infrastructure services.

  • Voluntary Intake Details: When you use our service intake tools, scheduling links, or inquiry portals, we collect identifying information such as your name, corporate or home address, phone number, email address, and a brief description of your technical requirements.
  • Technical Diagnostics: During active service provision, we may temporarily analyze hardware parameters, operating system versions, patch status, and network configuration metadata to resolve vulnerabilities or implement component replacements.

2. Strict Use of Information

Lockorix explicitly operates on a zero-monetization data policy. We do not sell, trade, lease, or distribute your private, corporate, or operational details to third-party marketing companies, advertisers, or external platforms. Your information is used strictly to communicate with you, coordinate local support appointments, and manage your defensive network profile.

3. Healthcare Compliance & Data Isolation

For our medical, dental, and healthcare clients, Lockorix operates with direct adherence to strict compliance parameters regarding sensitive data privacy.

  • Zero Local Storage: Our operations utilize secure, audited endpoints featuring hardware-based Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA). Our technicians maintain zero local storage of client Electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI) or proprietary business databases on physical diagnostic tools.
  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs): We strictly enforce legally binding downstream Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with our core operational software vendors, ensuring absolute data isolation across all network defense and remote support layers.
  • Log Retention: In line with standard industry frameworks, audit log data associated with network security assessments is securely retained to support compliance verifications and historical analysis.

4. Proactive System & Device Security

Because technical defense is our specialty, we secure our internal workflows using the same commercial standards we deploy for clients:

  • Full Disk Encryption: All technician workstations and endpoints utilize active Full Disk Encryption alongside centralized Mobile Device Management (MDM) software capable of instantaneous remote data destruction in an emergency.
  • Vulnerability Management: Our own operational environments are subjected to routine vulnerability scanning, automated patch management, and strict technical access controls to ensure your contact and network details remain entirely insulated.

5. Revisions and Contact Information

We periodically evaluate this policy to mirror updating regulations or operational improvements. If you have questions regarding our data privacy protocols, compliance procedures, or wish to verify your technical record status, contact us directly at derrick@lockorix.com.
