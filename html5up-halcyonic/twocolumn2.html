IT Support & Managed Security for Small Businesses

Protect Your Operations, Workplace Devices, and Business Data

Relying on patchy technology setups or waiting until a device breaks to call for help leads to costly operational downtime and missed deadlines. Lockorix Security provides dependable, local IT support and targeted cyber defense. We keep your daily computers, office hardware, and digital assets optimized and protected against modern business threats.

Hardware & Device Support

Maintaining the physical and digital tools your team uses daily.

Slow computers and unoptimized setups lower workforce productivity. We manage your office deployments, upgrades, and secure remote access to keep your systems fast and reliable.

  • Device Provisioning & Setup Sourcing, completely configuring, and deploying new workstations and business devices for seamless out-of-the-box integration.
  • Hardware Diagnostics, Upgrades & Replacement Running deep technical diagnostics to repair slow or broken machines, upgrading internal components, and swapping out parts to extend hardware life.
  • Business Wi-Fi Setup & Guest Networks Installing high-speed business routers and configuring entirely isolated guest networks so visitors get fast internet without accessing your private company data.
  • Secure Remote Access (VPN Setup) Configuring secure Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) so your employees can safely access office files from home or while on the road.

Threat Defense & Active Monitoring

Keeping your business data safe from hackers, ransomware, and malicious attachments.

Cybercriminals actively target small businesses because they expect easy, unmonitored vulnerabilities. We secure your environment and protect your workflows using proven, industry-standard defensive protocols.

  • Ongoing Network Monitoring Continuous, 24/7 technical oversight of your network infrastructure to ensure maximum uptime and immediate threat detection.
  • Automated Patch Management Keeping all operating systems (Windows and Mac) and core business software completely updated behind the scenes to fix security vulnerabilities.
  • Vulnerability Scanning Proactive system assessments to identify and patch security weaknesses before external threat actors can exploit them.
  • Malware Removal & Ransomware Protection Comprehensive detection, immediate isolation, and absolute elimination of malicious software alongside advanced protocol deployment to safeguard your critical records from encryption attacks.
  • Email Security & Anti-Phishing Filters Installing advanced spam and phishing blocks directly onto employee inbox accounts to stop dangerous emails before they get clicked.
  • Dark Web Monitoring Scanning breach databases to check if your employees' business email passwords have been leaked or sold on the dark web.

Data Continuity & Training

Insulating your records from permanent loss or human error.

Whether due to a sudden hardware crash, an accidental deletion, or an attack, losing business records can be fatal. We ensure your data is secure, backup-verified, and instantly recoverable.

  • Data Backup & Disaster Recovery Secure, automated backups with reliable restoration protocols to prevent permanent data loss and protect your critical history.
  • Employee Security Awareness Training Educational programs designed to teach your team how to confidently recognize phishing attempts and follow cybersecurity best practices.

Secure Technology Drives Business Success

Proactive IT management prevents expensive technical emergencies.

Don't wait for a workstation to crash, a wireless network to freeze, or a cyber threat to compromise your business accounts. Lockorix Security delivers honest, responsive local support and the practical everyday tools required to keep your business operating securely at peak performance.
