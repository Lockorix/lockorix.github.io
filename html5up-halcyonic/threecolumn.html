Enterprise-Grade HIPAA Compliance & Cybersecurity

Comprehensive Protections to Insulate Your Healthcare Practice

In healthcare, a data breach isn't just an IT headache—it's a massive legal liability, an operational crisis, and a direct threat to patient trust. Standard antivirus tools and occasional updates are no longer enough to meet federal mandates.

At Lockorix Security, we step in as your dedicated Business Associate. We deploy, monitor, and maintain a rigorous, legally defensible framework designed from the ground up to support your HIPAA compliance requirements and aggressively protect your electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI).

1. Complete Compliance Implementation & Alignment Blueprint

We don't patch over problems; we completely rebuild and harden your infrastructure. Our comprehensive implementation ensures your system directly aligns with strict federal framework expectations.

What is included in your implementation onboarding:

Technical Safeguards Implementation (§ 164.312): We engineer precise, unique employee access credentials, deploy automated workstation inactivity timeouts, configure enterprise encryption protocols for data at rest and data in transit, and activate advanced audit logging software across your entire network.

Physical & Environmental Hardening (§ 164.310): We establish formal protocols to lock down server environments, implement digital tracking and visitor logs, enforce physical workstation shielding from public view, deploy privacy screens, and configure hardware-level Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) alongside surge protections to secure your local medical equipment.

Administrative Framework Strategy (§ 164.308): We map out structured policy outlines to build your legally required 6-Year Retention compliance archive. We assist your team in executing formalized workforce security rules, structuring role-based information access levels, setting up security incident response blueprints, and formatting emergency contingency backup and disaster recovery architectures.

2. Continuous Proactive Guardrails & Active Managed Defense

True digital safety isn't a one-time setup; it's a continuous, daily operation. Once your clinical infrastructure is fully fortified, Lockorix shifts into an active defense posture—acting as your facility's personal digital bodyguard to continuously monitor, adapt, and maintain your pristine security status.

What is included in your premium ongoing protection:

Next-Gen Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR/MDR): Standard retail antivirus software is obsolete against modern hackers. We deploy advanced, enterprise-grade behavioral detection agents that actively identify, trap, and isolate ransomware threats before they can lock up your computers or spread to your network's secondary devices.

Continuous Risk Analysis & Vulnerability Patching: We run ongoing background digital threat assessments to expose hidden software vulnerabilities. We completely handle and coordinate rapid operating system, firmware, and application patches so you never have to worry about annoying update prompts or unpatched security holes.

Continuous Data Integrity & Bulletproof Backups: We manage multi-layer email security filters to shield you from sophisticated phishing scams and mandate secure Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA). To make sure your records remain untouched, we execute mandatory, recurring data backup restoration tests every 3 months to ensure your files are 100% recoverable in an emergency.

Interactive Staff Security Awareness Training: Technology is only half the battle; human error is how hackers get in. We provide direct, easy-to-understand security training for your staff. We teach them how to instantly spot advanced phishing emails, malicious text messages (smishing), deceptive phone scams, and social engineering tactics designed to steal credentials.

Behind the Scenes: Our Uncompromising Commitment

To safely handle your data, our own company operations are locked down under the exact same elite standards. Lockorix operates on a strict zero-trust baseline:

* Zero Shared Credentials: Every action taken by our engineers is strictly tied to unique, audited identities verified by hardware-based MFA tokens (YubiKeys).
* Proactive Mobile Protection: All technician endpoints feature Full Disk Encryption and Mobile Device Management (MDM) software capable of instantaneous remote data destruction.
* Strict Log Retention: We enforce absolute data isolation with zero local storage of client ePHI, absolute internal audit log data storage for 6 full years, and execution of legally binding downstream Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all of our core operational software vendors.
