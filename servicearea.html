Our Service Area

On-Site Technical Support & Remote Cybersecurity Across the Greater KC Metro Area

At Lockorix Security, we bring professional IT expertise and modern cyber defenses straight to your location. **Please note: We do not operate a physical retail repair storefront, and services are not performed at a Lockorix office. Instead, Lockorix comes completely to you, eliminating the hassle of unhooking your equipment, packing it into a vehicle, and driving it across town.

Two Flexible Ways We Support You

Depending on your technological needs, timeline, and location, we deploy our solutions through two highly efficient delivery models:

  • On-Site Mobilization (We Come to You): For complex hardware diagnostics, business Wi-Fi installations, device deployments, or workplace technical setups, we travel directly to your small business workspace, healthcare facility, or residential home.
  • Instant Remote Support (Secure & Virtual): If you are dealing with software patches, quick configuration fixes, or email filter updates, we can securely log into your environment remotely. This allows us to resolve technical bugs instantly, no matter where your staff is located.
